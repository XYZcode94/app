What is Limbo hacks?

Limbo Hacks is a 24 hour long hackathon scheduled on November 20th. A hackathon is an event where students, developers, designers, and entrepreneurs come together to create a project from scratch. Throughout the weekend participants will develop their ideas and build new technologies that will address our real-world problems. Along the way, they will connect with other tech enthusiasts, receive guidance from industry mentors, and much more!

What is a hackathon? +
A hackathon is a coding marathon where participants work to create innovative software or hardware projects from scratch within a limited timeframe, usually 24-48 hours.
Who can attend? -
Students from any educational background can participate. Whether you're a coding expert or just getting started, everyone is welcome to join and learn.
I have more questions! +
Reach out to our team through our contact page or social media, and we'll be happy to answer any other questions you might have!
How to register myself in the hackathon? +
Simply click on the "Register" button on our homepage and follow the instructions to complete your registration for the hackathon.
Am I to bring my own laptop? +
Yes, participants are required to bring their own laptops for the hackathon. Make sure it's charged and ready to go!
Can I volunteer? +
Yes! We welcome volunteers to help make the event run smoothly. Please contact us through our website for more information.
