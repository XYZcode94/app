About CODEXIS

Join us today and experience the magic of coding!



In an era where technology is advancing at an unprecedented pace, coding has become one of the most essential skills for students across all disciplines. Codexis, our college's coding club, aims to prepare students for the digital future. With innovative teaching methods and hands-on projects, we help students master the latest technologies.


Our mission extends beyond just teaching code. We believe in creating future-ready professionals who can adapt to the ever-evolving tech landscape. Through our peer-learning approach, students not only learn to code but also develop crucial collaboration skills that are essential in today's workplace.


What sets us apart is our focus on practical project experience. Members work on real-world projects, from artificial intelligence applications to data science solutions. Our alumni have gone on to work at leading tech companies, with impressive career trajectories.
